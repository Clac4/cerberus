send video link from chrome on Nexus 4 and 7 to xbmc


# make sure to configure the yatse widget (xbmc remote) 
# to connect to your computer running xbmc

1 - go to archive.org movie section

2 - find a movie you want to watch

3 - on the page with the video, look in the left hand sidebar for the http links to the mp4

4 - long click on the link to the video 

5 - this will open a pop up menu, select open in new tab

6 - the share menu will then appear, select send to xbmc to beam the video to xbmc
